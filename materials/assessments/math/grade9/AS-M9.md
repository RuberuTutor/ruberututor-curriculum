# Grade 9 Mathematics Assessment (AS-M9)

## Purpose
This assessment is designed as a **placement test** to identify a student’s readiness for Grade 9 Mathematics (MTH1W).  
It samples key **overall expectations** across all strands (A–F).  
Results guide tutors in determining the most appropriate **entry point for worksheets**.

---

## Structure
- **Format**: Mixed (multiple-choice, short answer, performance tasks)  
- **Length**: ~45 minutes  
- **Strands covered**: A–F (balanced coverage)  
- **Number of items**: ~20–25 questions total  

---

## Strand Coverage
- **Strand A (Mathematical Thinking and Making Connections)**  
  - Question(s) sampling problem-solving and making connections.  

- **Strand B (Number)**  
  - Question(s) sampling rational numbers, powers, proportions.  

- **Strand C (Algebra)**  
  - Question(s) sampling algebraic manipulation, equations, graph interpretation.  

- **Strand D (Data)**  
  - Question(s) sampling data representation and modelling.  

- **Strand E (Geometry and Measurement)**  
  - Question(s) sampling Pythagorean theorem, trigonometry, area/volume.  

- **Strand F (Financial Literacy)**  
  - Question(s) sampling decision-making and money concepts.  

---

## Scoring
- **Each strand contributes equally** to an overall placement score.  
- Rubric/answer key stored separately: `materials/assessments/math/grade9/AS-M9_key.md`.  
- Tutor notes: place student at **worksheet level** corresponding to lowest-performing strand.
