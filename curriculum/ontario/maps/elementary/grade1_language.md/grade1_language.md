## Strand B: Foundations of Language
### Overall Expectation
- Demonstrate foundational knowledge of oral language, reading, and writing systems.

**B1: Oral Language Foundations**
- B1.1: listen actively and respond appropriately in conversations  
- B1.2: speak to communicate ideas clearly and appropriately  

**B2: Reading and Writing Foundations**
- B2.1: apply knowledge of letters and sounds to read and spell simple words  
- B2.2: recognize high-frequency words in texts  
- B2.3: demonstrate basic print concepts (directionality, spacing, punctuation)  

**B3: Language Conventions**
- B3.1: construct simple sentences using basic grammar and capitalization  
- B3.2: use end punctuation correctly in simple sentences  

---

## Strand C: Comprehension – Understanding and Responding to Texts
### Overall Expectation
- Apply comprehension strategies to make meaning from a variety of texts.

**C1: Knowledge about Texts**
- C1.1: read and comprehend simple texts using knowledge of words, pictures, and background knowledge  
- C1.2: identify text forms (story, list, letter) and explain how they help convey meaning  
- C1.3: recognize simple patterns and features in texts (titles, pictures, labels)  
- C1.4: connect images and words to build understanding  

**C2: Comprehension Strategies**
- C2.1: activate prior knowledge before reading  
- C2.2: ask and answer simple questions during reading  
- C2.3: retell and summarize main ideas after reading  

**C3: Critical Thinking in Literacy**
- C3.1: express opinions about texts and provide a reason  
- C3.2: make personal connections between texts and experiences  
- C3.3: recognize whose voices are represented in a text  

---

## Strand D: Composition – Expressing Ideas and Creating Texts
### Overall Expectation
- Apply knowledge of text forms, drafting, and revising to create original texts.

**D1: Developing Ideas**
- D1.1: generate ideas for writing based on personal experiences and imagination  
- D1.2: plan texts using pictures, oral rehearsal, and simple notes  

**D2: Creating Texts**
- D2.1: draft short texts of different forms (personal narrative, simple persuasive, procedural)  
- D2.2: print letters and words with correct formation and spacing:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- D2.3: demonstrate personal voice by using words to express thoughts and feelings  
- D2.4: identify point of view (first person) in own writing  
- D2.5: make simple edits for capitals, punctuation, and spelling  

**D3: Publishing, Presenting, and Reflecting**
- D3.1: produce final texts using simple techniques to achieve an effect  
- D3.2: present texts aloud with expression  
- D3.3: identify a helpful strategy they used when presenting texts:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Cross-Strand Task (optional)
**Example**: *“All About Me Book”* – students brainstorm ideas (D1), draft pages with drawings and text (D2), apply phonics and sight words (B2), read aloud with expression (D3), and reflect on how they used transferable skills (A1).
